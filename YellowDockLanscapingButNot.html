About Us

At Cascadia GardenCraft, we bring the lush beauty of the Pacific Northwest's natural landscapes into your own backyard. Founded by a passionate plant enthusiast, our company is dedicated to creating breathtaking gardens that blend seamlessly with the region's native flora. With a deep knowledge of plants and an unwavering love for crafting outdoor sanctuaries, we specialize in designing gardens that not only soothe the soul but also provide bountiful harvests of fruits and vegetables.
